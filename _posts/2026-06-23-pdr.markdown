If you haven't seen it yet, check out the [Public Domain Review](https://publicdomainreview.org/)!

As they explain [here](https://publicdomainreview.org/about/), the PDR is a journal which aims "to promote and celebrate the public domain in all its abundance and diversity". They publish long-form essays every two weeks, covering all kinds of interesting and obscure topics. We only wish we'd known about them sooner!
